Research Overview

AI in Healthcare Hackathon 2026 — Central Asian University

Objective

Design AI systems capable of analyzing medical biopsy images through image classification (12 classes) and binary segmentation to detect regions of interest in tissue samples.

Classification Task

Multi-class classification of biopsy images into 12 categories (0–11). Disease names are intentionally hidden. The model predicts the correct numeric label for each tissue sample.
